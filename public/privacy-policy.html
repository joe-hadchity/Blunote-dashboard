1. Introduction

Welcome to Bluenote ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how Bluenote, a company specializing in AI-powered noise cancellation and real-time transcription services, collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our services, website, mobile applications, and related products (collectively, the "Services").

Bluenote is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This policy applies to all users of our Services, including individuals who visit our website, use our applications, subscribe to our services, or interact with our platform in any way.

Important Notice: By using our Services, you consent to the collection, use, and disclosure of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect various types of personal information that you provide directly to us:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, password, phone number, billing address, and payment information
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, job title, company information, timezone preferences, and language settings
  • Communication Data: Messages, emails, support tickets, and other communications you send to us
  • Meeting Data: Meeting recordings, transcripts, participant information, meeting schedules, and calendar integrations
  • Usage Preferences: Audio settings, noise cancellation preferences, transcription language preferences, and platform integrations

2.2 Audio and Voice Data

As an AI-powered audio processing service, we collect and process audio data:

  • Real-time Audio: Voice data from meetings, calls, and audio sessions for noise cancellation and transcription
  • Audio Recordings: Meeting recordings when enabled by users (with explicit consent)
  • Voice Characteristics: Voice patterns, accents, and speech characteristics for improving transcription accuracy
  • Background Audio: Environmental sounds and background noise for noise cancellation processing

2.3 Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use our Services:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, browser type, IP address, MAC address, and device identifiers
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on platform, click patterns, and interaction data
  • Performance Data: Application performance metrics, error logs, crash reports, and system diagnostics
  • Network Information: Connection type, bandwidth, latency, and network quality metrics
  • Location Data: General geographic location based on IP address (not precise GPS coordinates)

2.4 Third-Party Information

We may receive information from third-party services:

  • Calendar Integrations: Meeting data from Google Calendar, Outlook, Apple Calendar, and other calendar services
  • Communication Platforms: Data from Zoom, Microsoft Teams, Google Meet, Slack, Discord, and other platforms
  • Social Media: Information from social media platforms when you connect your accounts
  • Payment Processors: Transaction data from Stripe, PayPal, and other payment providers
  • Analytics Services: Data from Google Analytics, Mixpanel, and other analytics providers

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Service Provision

We use your information to provide and improve our Services:

  • AI Processing: Process audio data for noise cancellation, echo reduction, and real-time transcription
  • Account Management: Create and manage user accounts, authenticate users, and provide customer support
  • Feature Delivery: Enable platform integrations, calendar synchronization, and meeting management
  • Performance Optimization: Optimize audio quality, reduce latency, and improve transcription accuracy
  • Customization: Personalize user experience, preferences, and service recommendations

3.2 Communication and Support

We use your information to communicate with you:

  • Service Updates: Send notifications about service changes, new features, and maintenance windows
  • Customer Support: Respond to inquiries, resolve issues, and provide technical assistance
  • Marketing Communications: Send promotional materials, newsletters, and product announcements (with your consent)
  • Account Notifications: Send security alerts, billing notifications, and account-related communications

3.3 Business Operations

We use your information for business purposes:

  • Analytics and Research: Analyze usage patterns, conduct research, and improve our Services
  • Security and Fraud Prevention: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security threats
  • Legal Compliance: Comply with legal obligations, regulatory requirements, and law enforcement requests
  • Business Development: Develop new features, services, and business opportunities

We may disclose your information when required by law:

  • Legal Process: In response to subpoenas, court orders, or other legal processes
  • Law Enforcement: To assist law enforcement agencies in investigations

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: End-to-end encryption for audio data transmission and storage
  • Access Controls: Multi-factor authentication, role-based access controls, and regular access reviews
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection systems, and secure network protocols
  • Data Centers: SOC 2 Type II certified data centers with physical security controls
  • Regular Audits: Security assessments, penetration testing, and compliance audits
  • Employee Training: Security awareness training and background checks for employees

5.2 Data Processing Security

We ensure secure processing of audio data:

  • Local Processing: Audio processing occurs locally on your device when possible
  • Secure Transmission: All data transmission uses TLS 1.3 encryption
  • Temporary Storage: Audio data is stored temporarily and automatically deleted after processing
  • No Human Review: Audio data is processed by AI systems without human review unless explicitly requested

5.3 Incident Response

In the event of a security incident:

  • Immediate Response: We have a 24/7 security incident response team
  • User Notification: We will notify affected users within 72 hours of discovering a breach
  • Regulatory Reporting: We comply with applicable breach notification requirements
  • Remediation: We take immediate steps to contain and remediate security incidents

6. Data Retention

6.1 Retention Periods

We retain your information for different periods depending on the type of data:

  • Account Information: Retained for the duration of your account plus 3 years after account closure
  • Audio Data: Processed in real-time and deleted immediately after processing (unless you choose to save recordings)
  • Meeting Recordings: Retained for the period you specify (default: 30 days, maximum: 7 years)
  • Usage Data: Retained for 2 years for analytics and service improvement
  • Support Communications: Retained for 3 years for customer service purposes
  • Legal and Compliance Data: Retained as required by applicable laws and regulations

6.2 Data Deletion

You can request deletion of your data:

  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account at any time through your account settings
  • Data Export: You can export your data before deletion
  • Right to be Forgotten: We honor requests for data deletion under applicable privacy laws
  • Backup Data: Deleted data may remain in backups for up to 90 days
